Home Lab for Defensive Cybersecurity 🏠 💻 🧪 🔧

To gain hands-on experience in defensive cybersecurity, I built a custom home lab designed to simulate real-world enterprise environments. The setup includes virtualized endpoints and servers segmented across networks, protected by firewalls and monitored using advanced detection tools.

This lab enables me to practice critical blue team skills such as log analysis, threat detection, alert tuning, and incident response. By working with real traffic and simulated attacks, I’ve developed a strong foundation in monitoring, identifying, and mitigating cybersecurity threats in a controlled and practical setting.

Smart Bus 🚍 Route Finder (Path finding System) 🗺️⚡🔎

A Prolog-based route-finding system designed to calculate optimal paths between cities using multiple search algorithms—BFS, DFS, and A*. The system integrates real bus route data, includes safety checks for unsuitable inputs, and is structured for scalability and flexibility.

Extensive testing and refinement ensured accuracy, stability, and reliability across different scenarios. The project demonstrates how an academic concept was transformed into a practical, usable tool. 💡
